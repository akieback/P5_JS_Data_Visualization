Data Source



The data represents four years worth of data reported by California hospitals to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention National Healthcare Safety Network (NHSN). These datasets provided by California Department of Public Health identify Surgical side infections (SSI) in adult and pediatric patients after an operative procedure (CHHS, 2022). The datasets indicated important aspects about the hospital and operative procedures such as the facility id, county, hospital category, year, type of operative procedure, overall count of each procedure, and the corresponding reported Surgical Site Infections.
